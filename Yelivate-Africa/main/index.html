Incubation Zone of Impact

At Yelivate Africa, our mission is to empower individuals to lead a purposeful, happy, and fulfilling lives. Through technology, talent, and unity, we will raise our continent’s consiousness to create a sustainable African society that is free from poverty and mental health challenges.

Why Yelivate Africa?

What We Believe In

  • Individual's Potential

    Our inner strength and potential that we all want to realize and amplify.

  • Positive Contribution

    A genuine desire to live a thriving and fulfiling life and to help others.

  • Evidence-Based Approaches

    Strategies grounded in solid evidence and research that leads to success.

  • Synergistic Collaborations

    Working together with diverse partners amplifies our collective impact.

  • Transformative Relationships

    Building meaningful connections fosters personal and communal growth.
